**Playfab Unity Settings**
----------------------------------------

Download Unity PlayFabSDK from online


///*** Editor Settings **///

1)Click Windows in editor tab and select playfab editor extentions.
3)login in to your playfab account.
4)After login playfab go to settings and select studio name, titleid and save it...


///*** PlayFab Setting in Scene ***///

1)Click on playfabmanager object
2)Copy and paste the GameTitleID from your Playfab account.(Both Android and IOS)
